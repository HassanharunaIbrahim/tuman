About Us

Hassan Haruna Ibrahim Electronics & Tech Academy is a premier provider of electronics components, mobile accessories, and technology education, serving customers across Nigeria since 2010.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between technology products and education by providing high-quality electronic components, mobile accessories, and comprehensive training programs at competitive prices.

We source our products from reputable manufacturers and ensure each item meets our strict quality standards before reaching our customers. Our courses are developed by industry experts to provide practical, hands-on knowledge.

With our combination of quality products and education, we aim to empower the next generation of African technologists and entrepreneurs.
